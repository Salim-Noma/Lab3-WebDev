How to make pizza :

Making a pizza is as not difficult as you think. Just prepare a bowl, an oven, flour, salt, cooking oil, some water, sauce, mozzarella, mushroom, and bell paper. Then you can follow these three steps to guide you make a delicious pizza. First, put flour, salt, cooking oil, and water into bowl. Then knead dough using your own hand. After the dough blow up, roll it to make a circle. Second, we are going to make a topping. Grate a mozzarella, and cop mushrooms also bell paper. Next, spread the dough that you have made a circle with sauce and put mozzarella mushrooms and bell paper. You can arrange your own style for the toping. The last step, bake a pizza with an oven approximately 15 minutes. Finally your pizza has already to serve. In conclusion to make a pizza as delicious as in the restaurant is easy if you follow the three steps above. First make a dough, then prepare for the topping, and arrange it as beautiful as you can. Next bake it.
